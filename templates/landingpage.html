What is circuit?
Well, circuit is a website to post personal messages and stay in contect with other students of your university. Circuit matches the good things of google+ and twitter. You'll be able to post your own messages, find your friends and other students of your courses and you can add them to your circles.
Right, I know what you're thinking:
What are circles? Circles are groups of people, that have one interest. When you are logging in the first time, you will have the circle 'me' and the circle 'public'. You can build your own circles and friends can be added by the circle owner.
